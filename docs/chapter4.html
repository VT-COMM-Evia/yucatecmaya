Chapter 4

Puss in Boots now in retirement gets a few unwelcome guests with information that may just turn around Puss in Boots unfortunate life now.

Puss in Boots after giving up the life of a hero has gone into retirement. He soon gets some surprise guests looking to hire the legendary Puss in Boots, Goldilocks and the Three Bears. Goldi explains that they were hoping to hire Puss in Boots to steal the map to the wishing star that grants you any wish. But Baby Bear soon finds what looks to be the grave of Puss in Boots, they leave but this gives Puss in Boots the idea to steal it first to get his lives back.

As everyone does in retirement Puss grows out his beard and really lets that legendary figure go. In hiding from death, Goldi and the three bears show up hoping to be able to use Puss to steal the map from Big bad Jack Horner as Puss is a great thief. Puss at this point faked his death hoping to fool anyone looking for him and thankfully enough this group isn't the brightest and it works perfectly. But knowing there is a chance to get his 9 lives back, Puss needs to steal this map at any cost.
