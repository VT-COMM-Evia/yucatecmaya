Chapter 5

As Puss in Boots and Perro arrive at Jack Horner's, a new adventure is in store for our characters one that will change everything.

As Puss in Boots arrives at Jack Horner's, he successfully opens the chest and is about to get the map then an old friend Kitty Softpaws appears from inside the chest and steals it from Puss in Boots. As this is occurring Goldi and the three bears drop in from the ceiling and try to get the map from Puss in Boots and Kitty. Kitty steals the map and is escaping during all the ruckus. A retired Puss in Boots get hit by Goldi's staff and sends him directly into Kitty both of them falling perfectly into the waiting carriage Perro has below.

After breaking in perfectly Puss steals the map from right under Jack Horner's big nose and is ready to escape. His old flame Kitty Softpaws, who was also an ex-criminal, is waiting in the chest to steal the map as well. They fight over the map until Goldi and the Three Bears fall from the ceiling allowing them to escape the room. Making their way out Kitty and Puss, still fighting over the map, she gets the upper hand on the slow out of shape Puss and is about to leave until Goldi smacks Puss across the factory and knocks both of them out of the window.
